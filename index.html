My name is Zach and I am currently a senior at Middlebury College studying geography and computer science with interests in art history, sociology, and design.

I have worked with the Los Angeles Times Data and Graphics department, the Voting Rights Data Institute at Tufts and MIT, and as a research assisant for geography and gender studies professors at Middlebury. I am also a practicing artist working primarily in drawing, painting, and digital/generative techniques.
