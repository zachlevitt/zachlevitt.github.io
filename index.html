Cartography and GIS

Ridgecrest earthquake packed the power of 45 nuclear bombs, but its impact was muted

Designed and produced a map showing the location of the fault that ruptured during the July 5 Searles Valley earthquake near Ridgecrest, CA. Worked with earthquake reporter Ron Lin to visually explain why the earthquake's impact was muted due to rupturing on an axis directed away from population centers. Map published in print on page A1 of the Los Angeles Times.

Los Angeles Times, July 2019
