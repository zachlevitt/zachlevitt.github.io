What does climate change mean for where you live?

By 2100, climate change will have profoundly altered life in the United States. However, these effects will not be distributed equally across space. By integrating climate models with economic data, researchers have developed estimated economic damages from climate change for every county in the United States between 2080 and 2099. Use this map to understand the changes your county could experience and how they compare with the rest of the country.
